    About Me

    Currently at Apple as a Front End Web Developer creating visually awesome and technically challenging pages. It is an incredible feeling to know that the pages I help create are viewed by millions of people.
    When I started GuitarDistrict.com I had my first taste of how what I code can be used by anyone. I used HTML, CSS, Javascript, PHP in addition to working with API's (eBay, PayPal, Amazon, ProStores). It was a startup environment where smart thinking, meeting goals and customer service were a day in the life.
    At General Assembly I greatly expanded my knowledge by incorporating Ruby, Rails, jQuery, Git/GitHub (and much more) into my existing stack. It was unique to be in a truly immersive coding program where we were teachers and students, partners and leaders, thinkers and tinkerers.
